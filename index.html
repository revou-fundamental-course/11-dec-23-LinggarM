Our Profile

Welcome to SMAN 1 Antah Berantah

At SMAN 1 Antah Berantah, we are dedicated to providing a vibrant and enriching educational experience that empowers students to excel academically, grow personally, and contribute meaningfully to society. As a leading educational institution, we take pride in our commitment to excellence, fostering a supportive community, and preparing our students for a future filled with possibilities.

About Us

Established Legacy:

SMAN 1 Antah Berantah has a rich history of academic excellence and has been a cornerstone of education in our community for over 30 years. Our commitment to providing quality education has been unwavering, and our legacy is built on the success stories of our alumni who have gone on to achieve great heights in various fields.

Holistic Education:

At SMAN 1 Antah Berantah, we believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals. Our curriculum is designed to go beyond textbooks, emphasizing the development of critical thinking, creativity, and character. We encourage students to explore their interests through a wide range of extracurricular activities, from arts and sports to leadership programs.

Our Values

Integrity and Ethics:

We instill a strong sense of integrity and ethical behavior in our students. Upholding moral values is at the core of our educational philosophy, and we strive to develop responsible and principled individuals.

Inclusivity and Diversity:

Embracing diversity is part of the fabric of our school community. We celebrate the uniqueness of each student, fostering an inclusive environment where differences are respected and valued.

Community Engagement:

SMAN 1 Antah Berantah is not just a school; it's a community. We actively engage with parents, teachers, and the local community to create a collaborative and supportive learning environment. Together, we work towards the common goal of nurturing future leaders.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

Our campus features modern facilities that enhance the learning experience. From well-equipped classrooms to advanced laboratories and a library stocked with a diverse range of resources, we provide students with the tools they need to succeed.

Join Us on the Journey

We invite you to explore the opportunities that SMAN 1 Antah Berantah offers. Whether you are a prospective student, parent, or community member, we welcome you to join us on this exciting educational journey.

Vision & Mission

Vision of SMAN 1 Antah Berantah:

"To be a leading educational institution committed to nurturing future leaders with a passion for knowledge, creativity, and community service, empowering them to thrive in a dynamic and interconnected world."

Mission of SMAN 1 Antah Berantah:

Academic Excellence:

Strive for academic excellence by providing a holistic and innovative learning environment that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and lifelong learning.

Character Development:

Cultivate a values-based education that instills integrity, empathy, and resilience in our students, preparing them to face challenges with moral courage.

Global Awareness:

Foster global awareness and cultural sensitivity among students, encouraging them to embrace diversity and contribute positively to the global community.

Innovation and Technology:

Embrace technological advancements and encourage innovation in teaching and learning methods, ensuring our students are well-equipped for the demands of the 21st century.

Community Engagement:

Promote active community engagement and social responsibility, instilling a sense of civic duty in our students as they become compassionate and responsible citizens.

Extracurricular Enrichment:

Provide a diverse range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and leadership programs, to nurture the talents and interests of every student.

Collaborative Partnerships:

Establish strong collaborative partnerships with parents, educators, and the broader community to create a supportive and enriching educational ecosystem.

Environmental Stewardship:

Promote environmental awareness and sustainability practices, inspiring students to be responsible stewards of the environment for a better and greener future.
